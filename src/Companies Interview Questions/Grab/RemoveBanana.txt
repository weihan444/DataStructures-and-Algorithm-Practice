On each move, you can remove BANANA (1 B, 3 A, 2 N) from a String.
Given a string, output moves require to remove all BANANA from the String.

For example;

"BNAAANXX" ----1 move----> "XX" (1 B, 3 A, 2 N removed from the string)

Output:
1


Example 2:
"BNAAANXXBNANNXSD" ----1 move----> "XXBNANNXAASD" ----1 move----> "XXNXSD"

Output:
2
